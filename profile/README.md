<h1>Intravenous 2 - Overview</h1>

<h2>Purpose of the Game</h2>
<p><strong>Intravenous 2</strong> is a top-down tactical stealth action game for Windows that combines stealth, strategy, and action. The game lets players infiltrate hostile environments, make strategic decisions, and complete missions either quietly or with full firepower - depending on their play style. Released August 15, 2024 by developer <em>Explosive Squat Games</em> and publisher <em>HypeTrain Digital</em>, Intravenous 2 is designed for players seeking a challenging, immersive stealth-action experience.</p>

<h2>Main Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Dual Protagonists:</strong> You play as two characters - Sean and Steve - whose fates and stories intertwine, offering different perspectives and narrative paths.</li>
  <li><strong>Stealth or Firepower:</strong> Choose stealth - sneak through shadows, hack lights, pick locks - or go in guns blazing; the game adapts to your approach.</li>
  <li><strong>Customizable Arsenal & Gadgets:</strong> Dozens of weapons (with modifications like suppressors, laser sights), gadgets and ammo types allow varied strategies.</li>
  <li><strong>"Bullet Time" Mechanic:</strong> One protagonist (Sean) has the ability to slow time, giving tactical advantage in critical situations.</li>
  <li><strong>Responsive AI & Dynamic Encounters:</strong> Enemies react to your actions - take cover, call for backup, adapt - making stealth or combat tense and unpredictable.</li>
  <li><strong>Branching Story & Multiple Endings:</strong> Your choices - stealth or aggression, mercy or violence - influence story outcome and ending.</li>
  <li><strong>Mod Support and Workshop:</strong> Recent updates added Steam Workshop support, new weapons, and modding possibilities for custom content.</li>

</ul>
